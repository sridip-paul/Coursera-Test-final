Sushi

Nigiri - A topping usually fish, served on top of sushi rice. Sashimi - Fish or shellfish served alone (no rice). Maki - Rice and filling wrapped in seaweed. Uramaki - Similar to Maki, but rice here is on the outside and seaweed wraps around the filling. Temaki - Sushi that has been hand-rolled into a cone shape.
